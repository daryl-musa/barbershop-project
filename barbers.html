The Team

Meet the Barbers

Three chairs, three specialties. Pick whoever's free, or ask for someone by name.

Chair 1 · Owner

Steve Kamau

Steve founded the shop in 2019 after eight years cutting hair in Kayole and Eastleigh. Skin fades are his specialty, but he can do any style on request. He still works the chair most mornings before switching to running the business in the afternoon.

Specialty: Skin fades, line ups

Years cutting: 11

Usually working: Mon to Sat mornings

Chair 2

Brian Otieno

Brian joined in 2020 straight out of barber college, where he focused heavily on beard shaping and traditional hot towel shaves. If you want a full shave done properly, ask for Brian specifically. He also handles most of the shop's combo packages.

Specialty: Beard grooming, hot towel shaves

Years cutting: 6

Usually working: Tue to Sat, full days

Chair 3

Felix Mwangi

Felix joined in 2022 and brought experience the shop didn't have before, working with afros, locs, and natural hair textures. He trained under a specialist in Kasarani for two years before coming to Kabarnet Road. Book Felix if you're growing out locs.

Specialty: Afros, locs maintenance

Years cutting: 5

Usually working: Wed to Sun

A note on walk-ins and barber requests

We don't run an appointment system. If you ask for a specific barber and they're with another customer, you'll wait for that chair to open up rather than getting moved to whoever's free. Most days that's a fifteen to twenty minute wait. If you don't mind who cuts your hair, any open chair will do.
